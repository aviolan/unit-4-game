Pokemon Collector

You will be given a number to match at the start of the game.
There are 4 Pokemon below. By clicking on a Pokemon you will add a specific amount of points to your number.
You win the game by matching your number to the Computer's number.
You lose the game if your number goes above the Computer's number.
The value of each Pokemon is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Pokemon.
